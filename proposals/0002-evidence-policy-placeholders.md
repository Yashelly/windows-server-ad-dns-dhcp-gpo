# 0002 - Evidence policy and placeholders

## Evidence principles
- Evidence is used to show that key steps were performed and validated.
- Evidence is sanitized before publishing (usernames, serials, UPNs, object IDs, tenant IDs, public/external domains or IPs).
- Lab-only identifiers may remain for technical context (RFC1918 IPs, hostnames, `example.local`).
- Prefer focused screenshots and exports over full-screen dumps.

## Placeholders
- Empty folders may contain `.gitkeep` to keep the structure reviewable.
- Placeholders are acceptable while captures are planned.
- Status of evidence items is tracked in `STATUS.md`.
